How We Stay Free

Ad-supported, honestly

NexAlc is free because it runs Google AdSense ads. You'll see a few display ads on each page — that's how the lights stay on. We don't sell your data, require a login, or shove a newsletter in your face.

If an ad ever feels intrusive or breaks a tool, let us know. We'd rather lose an impression than break your experience.

Fine Print

What NexAlc is (and isn't)

NexAlc tools are for everyday estimates and quick calculations. They're not a substitute for professional financial, medical, or legal advice. Results may vary based on local conditions, rates, or data. Always verify anything important with a qualified professional.
