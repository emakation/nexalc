What We Are

A calculator suite that stays out of your way

Most calculator sites are either buried in ads, stuck behind a sign-up wall, or so cluttered you can't find the input field. NexAlc is the opposite: one focused tool per page, a clean interface, and results you can trust.

Every tool is built with real-world use in mind — splitting a dinner bill, estimating a mortgage, tracking a countdown to an important date. No unnecessary features. No dark patterns.

Our Methodology

How the numbers are calculated

Every formula on NexAlc is sourced from established financial, health, or mathematical standards — not guesswork. The mortgage calculator uses the standard amortization formula (M = P[r(1+r)ⁿ]/[(1+r)ⁿ−1]). BMI uses the WHO definition. Currency rates are pulled live from the Frankfurter API, which sources from the European Central Bank.

All tools run entirely in your browser — no server round-trips for calculations, no data sent anywhere. Results are instant because there's nothing to wait for.

How We Stay Free

Ad-supported, honestly

NexAlc is free because it runs Google AdSense ads. You'll see a few display ads on each page — that's how the lights stay on. We don't sell your data, require a login, or shove a newsletter in your face.
