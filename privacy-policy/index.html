Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 1, 2025  ·  Effective immediately

Short version: NexAlc doesn't collect personal information. We run Google AdSense ads, which use cookies. All calculations happen in your browser — nothing is sent to our servers.

1. Who We Are

NexAlc ("we", "us", "our") operates the website at nexalc.com, a collection of free online calculator tools. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our site.

2. Information We Do Not Collect

NexAlc does not require you to create an account, submit your name, email address, or any other personally identifiable information. All calculator inputs and results are processed locally in your browser and are never transmitted to our servers.

3. Information Collected Automatically

Like most websites, our hosting provider and third-party services may automatically collect certain non-personal technical information, including:

  • Your browser type and version
  • Your operating system
  • Referring URLs
  • Pages visited and time spent on the site
  • General geographic location (country or region level, derived from IP address)

This information is used in aggregate form to understand how the site is used and to improve our tools. It is not used to identify you as an individual.

4. Cookies

NexAlc itself does not set first-party cookies for tracking or login purposes. However, third-party services embedded on our pages — including Google AdSense and Google Analytics — may set cookies on your device.

You can control or disable cookies through your browser settings. Note that disabling cookies may affect how some features function. For more information on managing cookies, visit aboutcookies.org.

5. Google AdSense and Advertising

NexAlc uses Google AdSense to display ads. Google and its partners may use cookies and web beacons to serve ads based on your prior visits to our site or other sites on the internet. Google's use of advertising cookies enables it and its partners to serve ads to you based on your visit to NexAlc and/or other sites on the Internet.

You may opt out of personalized advertising by visiting Google Ads Settings or by visiting aboutads.info.

For more information on how Google uses data when you use our site, visit: google.com/policies/privacy/partners.

6. Google Analytics

We may use Google Analytics to understand how visitors interact with our site. Google Analytics collects information such as how often users visit the site, what pages they visit, and what other sites they used prior to arriving at NexAlc. We use this data solely to improve the site experience.

Google Analytics collects only the IP address assigned to you on the date you visit this site, not your name or other identifying information. We do not combine the information collected through Google Analytics with personally identifiable information. You can prevent Google Analytics from recognizing you on return visits by disabling cookies in your browser or by installing the Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on.

7. Third-Party Links

NexAlc may contain links to external websites. We are not responsible for the privacy practices or content of those sites. We encourage you to review the privacy policies of any third-party sites you visit.

8. Children's Privacy

NexAlc is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has submitted personal information to us, please contact us and we will take steps to remove it.

9. Data Security

Since NexAlc does not collect or store personal data, there is minimal security risk to your information. Our site is served over HTTPS to protect data in transit between your browser and our servers.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will update the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. Continued use of NexAlc after any changes constitutes your acceptance of the revised policy.

11. Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, you can reach us at: hello@nexalc.com
